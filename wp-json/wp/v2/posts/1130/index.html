\nWe’ve enjoyed hearing the coyotes and watching armadillos and many deer. The campground has a “no feeding” policy so while the deer aren’t especially afraid of people they do want to keep their distance. We also took advantage of the many pecan trees, picking up pecans for future use. By the way, if you are in the campground in the fall, check out the tangerine tree near the Activity Center. We picked a few of those too.<\/p>\n

We liked it at Colorado River Thousand Trails and look forward to future visits to this easy going campground.<\/p>\n
