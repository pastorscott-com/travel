Last year Jackie was looking at a denominational website where she saw a story about a retired librarian who had volunteered at Nazarene Theological College in Manchester, England for a year.\u00a0 At the bottom of that article there was a link to more information for others who might be interested in volunteering there.\u00a0 Thus began an interesting year of research, email exchanges, and many hours of discussion and prayers.\u00a0 The result was an invitation to come, provided a UK “Charity Worker Volunteer” visa could be obtained.\u00a0 Jackie would volunteer in the library 3-4 days a week and I would help out with the sound system and other things as needed.\u00a0 As volunteers we would receive no salary and would pay our own transportation and living expenses.\u00a0 An on-campus flat would be provided.<\/p>\n

We decided to go for it.<\/p>\n

As I have already written, we have been in the midst of winding down our fulltime RV lifestyle.\u00a0 What better time to begin a new adventure!<\/p>\n
