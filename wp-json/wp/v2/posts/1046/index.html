After having lunch we took a leisurely drive along Ocean View Blvd. stopping at several public access places along the way to watch the ships, wildlife, and the waves. The views are amazing and there are many places to stop and soak it all in.\u00a0 We saw more sea lions, a few dolphin, and a wide variety of birds.\u00a0 At Asilomar State Beach we took a stroll down to look at the tide pools.<\/p>\n
