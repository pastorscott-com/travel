\nWe’ve just finished another nice stay at Lake Conroe Thousand Trails, Willis, TX. One of the really nice features of this stay was the opportunity to be with good friends. Jerry and Carol were our Battleship Texas neighbors for several years. They were neighbors again, parked just behind us. Also Bob and Erma are local to Conroe and we invited them out for burgers and fellowship. It was a very nice night. Then long time friends Steve and Jeanette brought their new-to-them camper up for a few days. We were glad to have some time with them.<\/p>\n

Generally speaking mid-July temps in Texas keep people inside but our wet July produced some cooling thunderstorms several afternoons. Once the storm passed, it was nice sitting out.<\/p>\n
