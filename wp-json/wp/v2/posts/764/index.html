As we were driving back the 20 miles to the highway we stopped at Park Point Overlook which is at an altitude of 8,572 feet. We walked up the winding path to the fire lookout. There’s a wonderful view of the surrounding countryside. We especially liked seeing Shiprock Mountain in New Mexico off in the distance.<\/p>\n

Overall it was a strenuous day due to the altitude and ladder climbing but was fun and well worth the time and effort.<\/p>\n
