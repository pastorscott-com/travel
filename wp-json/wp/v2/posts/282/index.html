Another highlight from the area is the Pennsylvania Grand Canyon.\u00a0 This is a beautiful place for a day trip from where we were staying. \u00a0It was a scenic drive and we enjoyed looking at the historic houses and buildings \u00a0in the towns. \u00a0We did the mile and a half rim tour which was rather steep.\u00a0 The view of the river below was beautiful. The bike trail down through the canyon was tempting.\u00a0 It’s part of the “rails to trails” projects that are happening across the country where old rail lines are being removed and transformed into wonderful bike trails.\u00a0 We would have loved to ride the bike trail but we knew it would be too much for us. \u00a0We enjoyed walking in the woods and hearing the sounds of nature. This area was developed by the Civilian Conservation Corps and there is a statue in honor of these men.<\/p>\n

Scott and I have enjoyed this beautiful New York\/Pennsylvania stay and recommend it to all who love the beauty of nature.<\/p>\n
