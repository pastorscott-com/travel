Another beautiful town nearby is Oldenburg, IN which known as \u201cThe Village of Spires.\u201d There are, indeed, many impressive steeples around town. There is a beautiful historic section with many old original buildings from the 1800s. We enjoyed eating at Brau Haus restaurant. We missed the lunch special since it was sold out but the pork tenderloin sandwich with red cabbage was very good and big enough to be shared.<\/p>\n
