Nearby Placerville has a nice historic downtown with a variety of shops and eating places. We enjoyed going through the oldest hardware west of the Mississippi which has been open in the same location for 160 years. It has wooden floors, rolling ladders and something for everyone from appliances to plumbing supplies and paint. This street provides an idea of what early Placerville looked like.<\/p>\n

We enjoyed the area and took advantage of having a nice camp spot to relax and appreciate the natural beauty.<\/p>\n
