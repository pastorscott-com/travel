There are several attractions beyond shopping and eating. We walked through Nickelodeon Universe, an amusement park with a zip-line, several roller coasters, a flume ride, and a number of other rides for all ages. I can only imagine how popular this indoor venue is during the cold Minnesota winters. There is also the Sea Life Minnesota Aquarium and Moose Mountain Adventure Golf. In the exhibit area right now there’s the Barbie Dream House experience and a Star Trek exhibition. <\/p>\n

It’s easy to see why this is a must-see place for visitors in this area.<\/p>\n

Sorry to say because of some issues with the camper as well as some health-related issues we didn’t get to see several of the other area attractions we planned on seeing here. That will have to be done on a future visit!<\/p>\n
