\nWe spent an unplanned few days in Florence, MS, just south of Jackson, as we waited to have an alignment done on the motorhome.\u00a0 We landed at Wendy Oaks RV Park which is located right on Highway 49, around 12 miles south of I20.<\/p>\n

This campground is primarily a residential park but there are a few pull-throughs for travelers.\u00a0 These sites are reasonably easy to get into and long enough for bigger rigs.\u00a0 They are level, on gravel, and have 50 amp\/FHU connections.<\/p>\n

The main feature of the place is a pretty lake.\u00a0 A few campsites have nice views of it, most don’t but are just across the road from it \u2013 still a pleasant setting.\u00a0 There’s a block shower house with small laundry.<\/p>\n
