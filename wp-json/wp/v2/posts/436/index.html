I don’t think I’ve ever seen a nicer campground for tenting.\u00a0 There are many tent pads on the sides of the hill overlooking the lake.\u00a0 Most of them have steps with handrails leading up or down from the roads.\u00a0 The pads are level and compact, really tenting platforms; most with gorgeous views of the lake.<\/p>\n

As you can tell this is an interesting campground.\u00a0 It’s a challenge to negotiate but depending on exactly which campsite one is in it can be one of the prettiest spots imaginable.\u00a0 I guess it’s always true to some extent but here, more than most places, knowing the campground when making reservations makes a huge difference.\u00a0 There are spots here that I doubt I could ever get into and if I did, I probably wouldn’t like them very much.\u00a0 There are spots here that are absolutely wonderful and well worth the effort required to get into them.\u00a0 I give this place two thumbs up — depending!<\/p>\n
