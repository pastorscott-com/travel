On Saturday we attended ‘Early America Live’ in Jefferson, OH. \u00a0There were people in 1890’s period clothing who chatted with us “in character.”\u00a0 We enjoyed touring the restored Historic train depot and attended a service at the old Methodist church with a circuit riding preacher. The music for the song service was played on a pump organ by a local musician. \u00a0We also chatted with a woman who as dying yarn using local plants, flowers and walnuts and also a lady who was spinning yarn from various animals hair including buffalo. There’s also an 1800’s home, a post office, school, inn, and a pharmacy.<\/p>\n

On Sunday we enjoyed worshiping at the Jefferson, OH Church of the Nazarene.\u00a0 Our time in northeastern Ohio has been relaxing and interesting.\u00a0 It’s a pretty area with lots to see and do.<\/p>\n
