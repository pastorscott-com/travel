An easy “cruise-control drive” west on I10 returned us to Texas.\u00a0 We’re bound for a longer landing near Houston but for now we’re still in “approach” mode, spending a few days here near Livingston, then a couple of weeks in Conroe before doing our major stop for the winter.<\/p>\n

3. Maybe most impressive is the Care Center – it is an absolutely unique place providing assisted living facilities for people who retired to RVing but are now unable to travel.\u00a0 It’s a wonderful program that serves a great need.<\/p>\n

Our time here is short and, in addition to that, it has rained almost constantly since our arrival.\u00a0 Because of that we haven’t gotten to know the campground as well as we would have otherwise.<\/p>\n

Off hand, I’d say this place does just what it’s intended to do.\u00a0 It probably doesn’t measure up to some of the nicer RV Resorts, but no where is as geared to fulltime RVers as is this place.\u00a0 My guess is that we’ll return here about once a year for a short stay.\u00a0 After all, it is “where we live.”<\/p>\n
