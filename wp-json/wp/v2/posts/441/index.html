From there we went to Edgar Evins State Park to go up the 60 foot observation tower for a view of Cedar Hill lake. \u00a0The entrance to the observation tower is inside the Visitor Center so we went in and took the circular staircase up to the deck where I froze and held onto the center beam. \u00a0I could see a little of the lake and and dam from that spot and that was all the lake view there was. \u00a0Scott walked around taking other photos from the observation deck. \u00a0When it was time to go he went down the stairs in front of me so I could get down without too much embarrassment. \u00a0There were some lovely flowers still in bloom in the butterfly garden around the visitor’s center. \u00a0This looks like a beautiful park and has a marina, cabins, and camping available.<\/p>\n

Although we are out of the mainstream this has been another great week on the road!<\/p>\n
