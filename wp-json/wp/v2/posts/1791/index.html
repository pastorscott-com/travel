The water hookups are a bit strange.\u00a0 Four sites share a four hookup water spigot.\u00a0 In our case our water hookup was on the wrong side of the camper and fairly distant from it.\u00a0 I pieced a few spare hoses together to reach it, so no worries.\u00a0 However, I can imagine a person coming in and not having enough hose to reach.\u00a0 It would probably be wise to have some extra hose along.<\/p>\n

The campground has good WiFi and a heated bath house.\u00a0 I had good Verizon 4G and was able to get a satellite signal with no problem.\u00a0 A big Krogers and lots of other shopping is nearby.\u00a0 There are several seasonal residents at Finn Road and I can see why they like it.\u00a0 This campground gets a thumbs up from us.<\/p>\n
