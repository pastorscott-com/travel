Pine Ridge Campground is near I26, just south of Spartanburg, SC in the town of Roebuck.\u00a0 The route in includes a few zig zags on decent roads.\u00a0 The campground road itself is rather narrow and has a steeper, but very short hill.\u00a0 It is another of those campground entry roads that leave you hoping you don’t meet a RV coming in the opposite direction.\u00a0 Be careful, slow down, and look ahead to avoid a problem.<\/p>\n

Once in, the campground is nice with pretty good spacing for a private campground.\u00a0 There are some nice amenities: a good fishing lake, pool, playground, communal fire pit, and a jump pad for the kids.\u00a0 There is also a laundry and public area with comfortable chairs at the office.<\/p>\n

Most of the campsites have trees that provide some nice shade.\u00a0 I managed to bump into one and do some glass damage to the motorhome.\u00a0 It was driver error and not a fault to the campground.\u00a0 We will get the damage fixed on down the road in a week or so.<\/p>\n

Spartanburg has most any shopping you might want \u2013 and it is only a few minutes away.\u00a0 \u00a0I got satellite TV easily and our Verizon signal was good.\u00a0 The campground WiFi was okay, but it got pretty slow during the busy weekend.<\/p>\n

Aside from the too narrow road in and the trees that reach out and grab you (!) this is a nice spot for those traveling through the Spartanburg area.<\/p>\n
