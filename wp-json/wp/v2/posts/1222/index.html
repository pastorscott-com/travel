We’re spending a few days enjoying Blanco State Park, located in Blanco, TX not far from Austin and San Antonio. This is a smaller state park with the beautiful Blanco River flowing through it. The water is clear and cool – a great place to be on a hot summer day.<\/p>\n

The park has several full hookup RV sites — a very nice feature for a state park. Those sites\u00a0 go early, in fact, the park is quite full most weekends. If you want a full hookup site, make reservations — and make them early.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/div>\n

The park also offers screened shelters for camping. You can rent a shelter and then add a tent for a nice camping experience. The shelters, in fact, have the best overview of and access to the river.<\/p>\n

<\/div>\n

People love swimming, floating, fishing in, and, in general playing in the water.\u00a0Tip: Camp as close to the restrooms as possible for the best WIFI.<\/p>\n

<\/div>\n
This park is a real winner! We give it two thumbs up!<\/div>\n
