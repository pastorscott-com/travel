We’ve been in the Albuquerque, NM area a little longer than we intended to be but the weather was cold and very windy so we stayed put.\u00a0 While here we’ve enjoyed seeing some of the area.<\/p>\n

Monday was our last day in the area and we went to Old Town Albuquerque and enjoyed seeing the many shops with Native pottery, artwork, and jewelry.\u00a0 There are shops that will fit all tastes and budgets as well as a variety of restaurants, candy shops and bakery.\u00a0 The San Felipe de Neri church completed in 1793 is the center surrounded by a lovely courtyard and shops.\u00a0 We parked by the Art Museum and enjoyed all the sculptures in the area surrounding it.<\/p>\n

We have enjoyed our stay here with a view of the Sandia Mountains out our back window.<\/p>\n
