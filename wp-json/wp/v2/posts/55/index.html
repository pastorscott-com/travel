Name: Alvin Public Library<\/p>\n

Location: Alvin Texas<\/p>\n

Alvin Public Library<\/a> is part of the Brazoria County Library System<\/a>. It is located at 105 South Gordon in Alvin. \u00a0The staff is friendly, \u00a0willing to answer questions and help in any way they can. \u00a0\u00a0The building is nice and well kept. \u00a0It is well laid out making the collections easy to access. \u00a0 As you enter the library, the circulation desk is on the left with a copy machine and a welcoming \u00a0children’s area on the right. \u00a0There are computers available for the children and an alcove nearby for activities and story time. There are bright kid friendly bulletin boards and a beautiful mural on the ceiling of this area. \u00a0There are also several free public access computers available for adults who want to get on the internet. <\/p>\n

The building is well laid out with adult and non-fiction on one side with audios and Children’s materials on the the other. \u00a0At the back is a reading\/study area where newspapers and magazines are available for public use with comfortable seating. \u00a0It is a quiet refuge from a busy day or a friendly place to read.<\/p>\n

Revolving racks containing paperbacks for sale at a reasonable price are near the entrance for easy access for customer who are in the area temporarily \u00a0or going on vacation and are looking for something to read and not have to return.
