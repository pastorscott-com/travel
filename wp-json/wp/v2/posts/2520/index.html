\u00a0We have had a nice stay at Eisenhower State Park, Denison, TX.\u00a0 We came to Denison so I could fill in for a pastor friend while he took Sabbatical leave.\u00a0 The park is located on Lake Texoma and features a large marina and multiple camping areas.\u00a0 However, only the Bois D\u2019Arc Ridge loop has full hookups.<\/p>\n

We enjoyed the beautiful wooded campground with its herd of deer and other assorted wildlife.\u00a0 The neighborhood roadrunners are comical birds that are especially fun to watch.\u00a0 The campsites in this area are all pull-throughs, with each site a small loop off the road.\u00a0 That means everyone\u2019s back is to the road, creating a sense of privacy even when the campground is full.<\/p>\n

The biggest negative is that many of the sites are rather unlevel both side to side and front to back.\u00a0 Some are so bad that only a small camper has any reasonable hope of getting level.\u00a0 Also, in addition to bringing along plenty of leveling blocks be sure to bring extra sewer hose as the connection is seldom in a spot that will let you get level and be close to the sewer connection at the same time.\u00a0 And, even though the lake is very close by, there are no lake views to speak of.\u00a0 Toward the end of our stay the trees had dropped enough leaves that the lake could be seen through the forest from a few spots but there was nothing like a real lake view from this camping loop.<\/p>\n

I really should mention the hedge apples.\u00a0 Some of the sites have trees with this softball sized fruit on them.\u00a0 You certainly don\u2019t want to park your RV under one of those trees in the fall!<\/p>\n

I really don\u2019t mean for this to be a negative sounding review.\u00a0 We like this park a lot.\u00a0 The staff is friendly and easy to work with.\u00a0 Unless you arrive on a busy weekend, they will let you drive around and pick a site rather than stay in the one assigned.\u00a0 Also, if they know you are in a bigger rig they will try to assign you one of the longer, somewhat more level spots.<\/p>\n

The campground is just a few minutes from Denison with its restaurants, Walmart, and about any other business you might need.<\/p>\n
