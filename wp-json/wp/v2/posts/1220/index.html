<\/a>As I\u2019ve said before, Brazos Bend State Park, just south of Houston might be called our \u201chome park\u201d and we\u2019ve just spent a few nice days there.\u00a0 Here in the mid-June we\u2019re well into summertime temps with highs in the mid 90\u2019s.\u00a0 I\u2019m amazed at the number of people who tent in these temps.<\/p>\n

<\/a>For Jackie and me, tenting days are long gone \u2013 especially on hot summer days.\u00a0 Our Jayflight is just fine, thank you!<\/p>\n

We do our exploring early and then stay inside under the a\/c.\u00a0 Jackie likes to read and I’ll watch a baseball game or a movie.\u00a0 Then, as evening falls we do more outside stuff.<\/p>\n

<\/a>One nice nighttime feature of Brazos Bend is the George Observatory<\/a>.\u00a0 If you get $5 tickets you can look through the big telescopes.\u00a0 Otherwise, just head for the observatory and enjoy looking through smaller scopes set up by the local astronomy club.\u00a0 On Saturday night we got some terrific views of Saturn, rings and all.<\/p>\n

<\/a>Of course, there\u2019s lots of wildlife to see.\u00a0 I know I have many alligator photos but every time we visit, I\u2019m compelled to take a few more.\u00a0 We enjoyed watching an armadillo digging around our campsite one night.\u00a0 It wasn\u2019t too concerned about me following it around taking it\u2019s photo although it finally got spooked and headed off into the nearby underbrush.<\/p>\n

If you like bicycling, Brazos Bend is the best!\u00a0 It\u2019s flat with beautifully maintained trails.\u00a0 You\u2019ll see lots of families out on bikes.\u00a0 We get a kick out of watching dads peddling along, pulling \u201crickshaw-like\u201d trailers for the little ones.<\/p>\n

<\/a>
\nFinally, I\u2019ll mention the nifty shelters.\u00a0 There are several screened shelters in one area.\u00a0 Each one has electricity and a water faucet (on the outside).\u00a0 Some people park their popup campers next to them (there are large, paved parking areas at the shelters) and plug into the outlet.\u00a0 Doing so, they\u2019ve turned their popup into a \u201cbig rig\u201d with a large screened in room.<\/p>\n
