Jackie and I have talked about making a trip to London and\/or Paris for years.\u00a0 Of course, it\u2019s a big deal financially so it\u2019s mostly been something to talk about while not really expecting to do it.\u00a0 A few months ago we talked about it a bit more seriously.\u00a0 We know that retirement may not be real soon, but it is lurking out there.\u00a0 We know we won\u2019t be able to afford such a trip as retirees so, in some ways it is now or never.<\/p>\n

I\u2019ve kept an eye on airfares and they were always too high.\u00a0 We\u2019d pretty much given up on the trip for this year, hoping airfares would drop before our eventual retirement.\u00a0 Still, I\u2019ve kept an eye on fares, and a few months ago, I happened to think of it and checked.\u00a0 To my surprise they had dropped by quite a bit.\u00a0 It was still hard to commit to such a trip, but, after thinking about it a few days we decided to go for it.\u00a0 The next day, the airfares went right back up and never came down again.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/div>\n

Our friends cheered us on \u2013 I especially appreciate the support from the good church I pastor.<\/p>\n

So, on Sunday afternoon we headed for IAH for a 9 hour flight to London Heathrow.\u00a0 We flew British Airways \u2013 economy class.\u00a0 It wasn\u2019t bad, although it would, of course, be nice to sit up front in one of the fancy seats.\u00a0 The flight leaves at 4:20pm Houston time, but it\u2019s already 10:20pm in London.\u00a0 If the traveler can trick their body into sleeping on the flight it\u2019s a very good thing.\u00a0 Both Jackie and I got some sleep \u2013 or at least we dozed a bit.\u00a0 Still when the flight arrived at 7:00am local time, our bodies thought it was 1:00am.\u00a0 A six hour time difference is nothing to sneeze at!<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/div>\n

Sleeping on the flight would have been a bit easier had it not been for a church tour group on the flight.\u00a0 They were headed for Athens and in a festive mood.\u00a0 As we tried to sleep a group gathered around the seat right behind us for good natured chatter.<\/p>\n

Still, it wasn\u2019t too bad.\u00a0 We had already planned to take it easy the first couple of days anyway.\u00a0 It took about 2 hours to clear UK immigration and then another hour of riding on the famous London Tube to our destination \u2013 a nifty studio apartment near Canary Wharf.<\/p>\n
