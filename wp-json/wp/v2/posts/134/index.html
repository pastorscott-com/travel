<\/p>\n

The main attraction here, aside from the beauty of the place, is fishing.\u00a0 The state is encouraging fishing, in fact, there are fish feeders stationed around the lake.\u00a0 We’ve seen lots of people fishing and with some success.\u00a0 There’s a nice trail the circles the lake, probably an hour’s walk all the way around.\u00a0 The roads here are excellent and just right for bike riding.<\/p>\n

This park is a real winner and we recommend it to folks traveling up I35 between Emporia and Kansas City.\u00a0 It will go on our “let’s go back” list.<\/p>\n
