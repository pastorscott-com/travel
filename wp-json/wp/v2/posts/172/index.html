Jackie writes…<\/p>\n

Deere & Company World Headquarters on One John Deere Place is a beautiful building designed by architect Eero Saarinen.\u00a0 It is set in wooded surroundings and is beautiful.\u00a0 In the lower level there are more antique tractors and plows along with heavy equipment and large harvesters. I am amazed at the size of the some of the equipment used now when compared to the old tractors and plows that were pulled by horses or oxen.\u00a0 \u00a0We saw the golden plow that was at various World fairs and trade shows.\u00a0 Along one entire wall the history of the Company is shown with other historical memorabilia starting in the 1800\u2019s.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

We had a lot of fun visiting John Deere and recommend it as a great place to spend an afternoon.<\/p>\n
