We really like our NuWa Hitchhiker II LS.\u00a0 Even though the company is no longer manufacturing campers the brand has a good reputation, especially among fulltime RVers.\u00a0 The company did a lot of things right on our camper but it’s selection of water faucets wasn’t one of them.\u00a0 The camper was built with very cheap plastic water faucets in both the kitchen and bathroom.<\/p>\n

Last night we stopped off at the store and picked up a couple of replacements.\u00a0 These aren’t high end, expensive units, but they are a great improvement over the originals.\u00a0 We greatly appreciate our friend Ron who came by and spearheaded the project.\u00a0 In the photo below you’ll see the old kitchen faucet on the left and the new one on the right.\u00a0 Looks good doesn’t it.<\/p>\n
