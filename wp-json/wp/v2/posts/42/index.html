Location: Danbury, Texas<\/p>\n

Danbury Public Library is a branch of the Brazoria County Public Library System<\/a>. \u00a0It is located between Angleton and Alvin Texas off of highway 35. \u00a0As I went into the building I was welcomed by the staff and their attitude was one of readiness to help me in any way. \u00a0They are making excellent use of the space they have. \u00a0The collection is easy to use and The bulletin boards are fresh and current.<\/a><\/p>\n

There is an activity room used for story time and other library activities. \u00a0If you are interested in local history there a many pictures in this room showing the history of Danbury.<\/p>\n

As I was leaving I noticed a rotating book rack with paperbacks for sale to those who prefer to purchase rather than borrow books. \u00a0I plan to return and look over the paperbacks again.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
