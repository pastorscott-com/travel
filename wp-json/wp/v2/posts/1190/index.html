RVing and carpet don’t go well together. \u00a0We’ve found\u00a0keeping the carpet clean\u00a0to be a constant battle. \u00a0After considerable research we decided to go with Allure Trafficmaster laminate from Home Depot. \u00a0I’m not especially talented with it comes to stuff like this and from the Youtubes I watched and the reviews I read this project sounded like it was within my range of capabilities.<\/p>\n
