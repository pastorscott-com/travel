Our guide walked us over to another building called Hanger Bay One where we saw modern day aircraft including a Marine One Presidential Helicopter, and also airplanes used in Korea, Vietnam, and Dessert\u00a0 Storm.\u00a0 The Coast Guard\u00a0Aviation Exhibit was interesting with an HH-52 Seaguard and photos of early coastguard aviators.<\/p>\n

The most striking exhibit for me was the Blue Angels and the American Flag. For families with small children there is a play area where they can run free and not hurt themselves.<\/p>\n

Again, this was our third visit to this great museum and there’s a good chance that we will return on future visits to this area.<\/p>\n
