We\u2019re impressed with Poche\u2019s RV Park and Fish-N-Camp at Breaux Bridge, LA \u2013 near Lafayette.\u00a0 The route in from I10 includes a few rough roads and several turns, so you want to check out the directions to the campground.\u00a0 Once you arrive you\u2019ll see a property with five 10 acre lakes.\u00a0 There\u2019s a paved road around one of the lakes and alongside of another with back in campsites all along the way.\u00a0 In this campground just about everyone has a lake-front site!\u00a0 The road around the lake is mostly good, but has several rough sections where the big rigs have broken up the pavement.\u00a0 That\u2019s the only real negative I can think of.\u00a0 The lady who checked us in was very welcoming and accommodating.\u00a0 Our site was paved and mostly level.\u00a0 We had fairly good WiFi and full hookups.\u00a0 I got a kick out of seeing kids catch fish (and a lot of adults were fishing too).\u00a0 Early in the week the campground honors several discount cards, making it a very good deal on those nights.\u00a0 This campground will be on our list for return visits as we travel across Louisiana on I10 in the future.<\/p>\n
