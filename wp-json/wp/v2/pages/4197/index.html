<\/a>We are Scott and Jackie Cundiff.\u00a0 Jackie is a retired librarian and Scott is a retired pastor.\u00a0 We sold our house, bought a big pickup and camper and began a great adventure of traveling and living in our RV full time.\u00a0 We fulltimed for 9 years before selling the rig and embarking on another adventure.<\/p>\n

In July of 2022 we began a new Adventure, embarking on a year-long stay in Manchester, England where we volunteered at Nazarene Theological College.\u00a0 These days we have “retired” from our travels and are comfortably settled down near family and friends. We write about our adventures here.<\/p>\n

This website is where we share our travels with all who are interested.\u00a0 You’ll find campground reviews, photos of some of the interesting places we visit, and general fulltime RVing information here.\u00a0 Even though we are no longer fulltiming, some of the information you will find here about the RV lifestyle will be helpful.<\/p>\n

Scott’s pastoral writing is at Daily Devotional Writing from Pastor Scott.<\/a><\/p>\n

Our last fulltime rig was a 2005 Safari Cheetah motorhome that we bought in January, 2019.\u00a0 Our idea of roughing it is being in a campsite where we can’t get satellite TV or, even worse, where we don’t have Internet access!<\/p>\n

<\/a>For the first 6 years of our fulltiming adventure our tow vehicle was a 2008 F-350 and our camper was a 2007, 35 foot NuWa Hitchhiker II LS with three slide outs.<\/p>\n

In July of 2022 we began a new Adventure, embarking on a year-long stay in Manchester, England where Jackie is volunteering in the library of Nazarene Theological College and Scott is volunteering as needed.<\/p>\n
